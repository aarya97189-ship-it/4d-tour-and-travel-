Standard 9 Nights / 10 Days Route

Day 01: Haridwar/Dehradun to Barkot

Scenic drive to Barkot via Mussoorie. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 02: Barkot - Yamunotri - Barkot

Trek to the holy Yamunotri Temple, take holy dip, and return to Barkot.

Day 03: Barkot to Uttarkashi

Drive along the Bhagirathi river to reach Uttarkashi. Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple.

Day 04: Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi

Drive to Gangotri Dham through Harsil valley. Perform Pooja and return.

Day 05 & 06: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi & Kedarnath Trek

Proceed to Guptkashi/Sonprayag. Next day, begin the sacred trek to Lord Kedarnath.

Day 07 & 08: Kedarnath to Pipalkoti & Badrinath

Descend from Kedarnath, proceed towards Joshimath and onward to Badrinath Dham.

Day 09 & 10: Badrinath to Rishikesh / Dehradun Departure

Return journey via Rishikesh sightseeing. Departure with divine memories.
